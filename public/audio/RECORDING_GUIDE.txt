Twi Voice Recording Guide for Farroway
========================================

Place native Twi speaker recordings in the tw/ subdirectory.
Filenames must match the prompt IDs in src/services/voicePrompts.js.

Recording specs:
  Format:      MP3
  Sample rate:  16 kHz or higher
  Channels:    mono
  Bitrate:     64-128 kbps
  Duration:    2-5 seconds per clip
  Style:       clear, warm, unhurried ‚Äî spoken to a farmer, not read aloud
