# AgriPilot Pilot Pitch

---

## 30-Second Version

"AgriPilot is a digital verification platform for agricultural credit programs. When a field officer registers a farmer and submits a loan application, AgriPilot captures GPS coordinates, maps the farm boundary, hashes every photo for fraud detection, and scores the application automatically. It catches duplicate photos, shared devices, and overlapping GPS coordinates before a single shilling is disbursed. We are looking for a partner to run a pilot with five to ten field officers for one lending cycle."

---

## 1-Minute Version

"Most agricultural lending programs in East Africa still use paper forms and manual verification. Field officers collect data on paper, GPS coordinates are logged by hand, and fraud detection depends entirely on individual reviewers remembering previous applications. The result is slow disbursement, missed fraud, and limited accountability.

AgriPilot replaces that with a digital workflow. A field officer opens the app on an Android phone, registers a farmer, captures GPS coordinates with device-level accuracy, walks the farm boundary, and uploads photo evidence. Every photo is hashed so the system can detect if the same image appears in a different application. The platform scores each application on GPS quality, boundary accuracy, evidence completeness, and farmer profile — and flags anything suspicious.

Reviewers see a verification queue with scores and fraud flags. Administrators see a portfolio dashboard with real-time decision analytics. Every action is logged in an audit trail for compliance and donor reporting.

We have built this, it is live, and it works today. What we are looking for is an institutional partner — an NGO, lender, or cooperative — to run a structured pilot. Five to ten field officers, one lending cycle, full support from our team. The goal is to demonstrate the difference digital verification makes on real applications in your program."

## Pilot Ask

"We are proposing a 90-day pilot with the following structure:

- **Scope:** 5-10 field officers in one region
- **Setup:** We create accounts and configure the system for your workflow in one day
- **Training:** One 60-minute training session for field staff, one 30-minute session for reviewers
- **Support:** Dedicated support during the pilot period
- **Data:** You own all data collected during the pilot
- **Cost:** No cost during the pilot period
- **Success metric:** Compare verification time, fraud detection rate, and audit completeness between AgriPilot and your current process

At the end of the pilot, we review the results together and decide on next steps.

Can we schedule a follow-up to define the pilot scope for your program?"
