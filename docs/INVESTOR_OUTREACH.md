# Farroway — Investor Outreach Templates

Copy-paste-ready emails for the four standard outreach moments.
Replace bracketed slots before sending. Subjects are designed for
mobile previews — no more than 60 characters.

---

## 1. Cold outreach

> **Subject:** Last-mile decision tool for 500M smallholders — quick read?

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Investor's Firm]'s thesis on [climate
agriculture / emerging markets / mobile-first SaaS] — particularly
your investment in [portfolio company].

I'm building **Farroway**, a daily decision + marketplace surface
for smallholder farmers across Africa, India, and US backyard
markets. Six markets are already configured; the live demo runs
fully offline.

Three things I'd love your read on, in under 5 minutes:

1. The pitch deck (one-pager): [link to FUNDING_PITCH.md]
2. The live demo: [staging URL] — try the `/buy` route after
   switching markets via the chip on `/home`.
3. The metrics dashboard at `/internal/metrics` — live funnel
   per market.

Happy to send a Loom walkthrough if that's easier than the demo.

Thanks for considering,
[Founder Name]

## Cadence

| Day | Action                                      |
|-----|---------------------------------------------|
| 0   | Cold outreach OR warm intro                  |
| +7  | Follow-up #1 — one concrete update          |
| +21 | Follow-up #2 — close the loop, no pressure  |
| —   | Decline-graceful when the pass arrives      |

Each step has a clear next action; nothing in the sequence is
ambiguous about the ask. The metrics dashboard is the proof.
